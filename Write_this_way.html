Useful writing tips

Do you have writers block and looking for some inspiration again?

Here is a few great tips and helpful advise for you. Firstly grab a piece of paper and pen, or if you would like a word document on your laptop or computer. Write the first word that comes into your mind

Then write other words surrounding the first word, you can describe the word you wrote or link other ideas with it, from here you can form a starting point for your story.

Helpful tips:

Have your own space, peace and quiet can help your creative mind

Surround yourself with things that once used to inspire you, maybe it can inspire you again.

Read through some of your past work, maybe add to it? or it can inspire you to write something similar or different. Test how different your writing is from back then to now.
